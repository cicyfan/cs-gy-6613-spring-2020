    Project 2 - Continual Learning for Robotic Perception

    One of the greatest goals of AI is building an artificial continual learning agent which can construct a sophisticated understanding of the external world from its own experience through the adaptive, goal-oriented and incremental development of ever more complex skills and knowledge. Continual learning is essential in robotics where high dimensional data streams need to be constantly processed and where naïve continual learning strategies have been shown to suffer from catastrophic forgetting.
